Skin Care Guide ♥

Skin care is the range of practices that support skin integrity, enhance its appearance and relieve skin conditions.

It involves the use of skincare products such as cleansers, moisturizers, sunscreens, and treatments for specific skin concerns.

First and most important step before starting a skincare routine is identifying your skin type.

Different Skin types:

  • Normal
  • Sensitive
  • Dry
  • Oily
  • Combination

How to identify your skin type?

Follow the below given steps to know your sking type at home.

  1. Wash your face - Gently cleanse your face with mild face wash and pat it dry.
  2. Wait for an hour - Don not apply any moisturizer, toner or serum. Let your skin return to its natural state
  3. Observe how your skin feels -
    • Normal Skin: Your skin feels balanced, neither too oily nor too dry.
    • Sensitive: Your skin feels itchy, red or irritated.
    • Oily skin: Your face looks shiny, especially on the forehead,nose and chin.
    • Dry Skin: Your skin feels tight, rough or flaky.
    • Combination: Your T-zone is oily but cheeks are dry.
